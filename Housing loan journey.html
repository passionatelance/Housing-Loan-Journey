Loan Requirements

General Requirements

  • 2 Valid IDs
  • Completed application form

Income Documents

  • Latest Certificate of Employment
  • Latest three (3) months' pay slips
  • Latest three (3) months' bank statement
  • 2024 Income Tax Return
  • Authorization letter for verifications

Collateral Documents

  • Contract to Sell
  • Latest Statement of Account from developer
  • Letter of endorsement
  • Official Computation sheet

Post-Approval Requirements

  • PSA Marriage Certificate/PSA CENOMAR with official receipt
  • Any BPI account (existing/new) for auto debit
  • Two (2) Valid Government Issued IDs with signatures
  • 2024 Income Tax Return

  • Original copy of PSA Certified CENOMAR with receipt
  • Automatic Debit Authorization Form
  • Confirmation to debit bank charges

Loan Process Flow

1

Document Submission

Submit all required documents for evaluation.

2

Initial Evaluation

Account undergoes credit evaluation and appraisal

3

Final Evaluation

Credit team grants approval based on income and property value.

4

Loan Signing

Sign loan documents and complete post-approval requirements.

5

Bank Guaranty Issuance

Bank issues Guaranty to Developer (3-5 working days upon receipt of signed loan documents and complete post-approval requirements).

6

Loan Release

Once letter of undertaking and originally signed bank guaranty is returned, loan released will be processed within 7-10 banking days.

Key Conditions

Promo Conditions

  • Subject to terms of tie-up agreement with developer
  • Loan proceeds must be released before promo due date
  • No individual takeout of titles

Insurance Requirements (If to endorse)

  • Original copy of Life/Fire Insurance Policy
  • Deed of Assignment to Bank
  • Original Official Receipt of latest premium payment
